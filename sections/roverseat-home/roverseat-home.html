Questions? We've got answers

Everything you need to know before your next ride.
Will it fit my car?

Our covers use adjustable straps and anchors that fit the vast majority of sedans, SUVs, and trucks sold in the U.S.

Is it actually waterproof?

Yes. Every cover uses a sealed Oxford top layer with a waterproof lining, so mud, rain, drool, and accidents stay on top and wipe right off.

Can my dog still be buckled in?

Absolutely. Each cover includes seat-belt openings so you can thread a harness or safety leash through and keep your pup secured.

How do I clean it?

For everyday messes, just wipe it down with a damp cloth. For a deeper clean, unclip the cover, machine wash on cold, and hang to dry.
